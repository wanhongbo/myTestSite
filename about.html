About TickFlow

Version: 1.0.0

TickFlow is a simple and focused productivity application designed to help you capture tasks, organize priorities, and stay on track every day.

What We Offer

  • Task creation and editing with clear priorities.
  • Categories and scheduling support for better planning.
  • Reminders to help you never miss important work.
  • Weekly insights to review your progress.

Our Mission

We believe productivity tools should be fast, private, and easy to use. TickFlow focuses on practical features without unnecessary complexity.

Platform Support

TickFlow is built with Flutter and supports both Android and iOS.
